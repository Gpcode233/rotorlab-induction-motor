CONTROL MODEL

PID speed regulation

u(t) = Kp·e(t) + Ki∫e(t)dt + Kd·de(t)/dt

The proportional term reacts to present error, integral removes steady-state error, and derivative limits rapid oscillation. The controller adjusts the simulated VFD command.

SIGNAL CONDITIONING

Finite impulse response filter

y[n] = Σ b[k]x[n-k]

Seven equal coefficients average recent sensor samples. FIR filters are inherently stable because they have no feedback and reduce high-frequency measurement noise.
